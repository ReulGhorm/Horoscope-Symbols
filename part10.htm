The Role of the Symbols‌

Turning to the horoscope symbols themselves, now, we will look at

the range of patterns associated with each. We must understand that we are dealing not with forces that coerce us, but with psychological, spiritual, and metaphysical energies that lie both within ourselves and within the universe. In every aspect of our lives we will manifest these forces: in our bodies, our minds, our ways of life, our relationships with others, and even in what we see around us.

It is our sharing of these energies with the universe that ties us to it, makes us part of it, and ensures that it is our home. Within these patterns our creativity and responsibility are not diminished, but only given a form from which infinite possibilities can emerge.
