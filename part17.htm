The Planets: Introduction


As a prelude to the individual discussions of the planets in the next chapter, I introduce the solar system here as a whole, showing how it is a framework for the symbolism of the individual planets. When you come to the longer planet discussions, you will then have a better idea of how each planet fits into the overall symbolic structure of the solar system. We will view the solar system from a broad perspective, considering both the astronomical and the symbolic realities: not only the objective structure, but also the manner in which the human psyche perceives it.
